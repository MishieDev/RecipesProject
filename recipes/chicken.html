Crispy chicken thighs

Home Fatty Liver Skilpadjies Scrumptious lamb chops

Golden skin, juicy bites—these chicken thighs make a bold, crave-worthy case for poultry on the carnivore plate.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons duck fat or tallow

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels
  3. Season generously with salt and pepper
  4. Heat fat in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Place thighs skin-side down and cook until golden, about 5 minutes
  6. Flip thighs and transfer skillet to oven
  7. Bake for 15–18 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F
